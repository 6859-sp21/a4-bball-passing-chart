Tracking Ball Movement in the NBA: A Data Visualization

We dissected the 2015-2016 NBA season and asked: given a player with the ball in a certain location on the court, what comes next?

NBA players and do one of 3 things with a basketball: (1) dribble, (2) pass, and (3) shoot. While many existing visualizations effectively depict shot percentages, few explore ball movement. Here, we take a closer look at ball movement through the lens of passes and shot percentages. Click a spot on the court below, and see the heat map of possible pass locations.
